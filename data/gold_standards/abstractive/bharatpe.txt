 Former Bharatpe chief Ashneer Grover has written to the company's board seeking action against CEO Suhail Sameer following his offensive remarks on social media. He has demanded a written apology from Sameer and in absence of the same, he and his sister may seek damages and pursue criminal defamation against the CEO and the company. Grover was sent on leave for allegedly misbehaving with an employee of Kotak Mahindra Bank.
